Privacy Policy

Last Pick · Last updated: July 2026

Last Pick ("we," "the app") helps you decide what movie to watch. This policy explains what we collect and why. We keep it short because we keep it simple.

What we collect

  • Account info. If you create an account, we store your username and (for accounts that use one) your email address, so you can log in and sync across devices. Accounts are optional — you can use the app as a guest.
  • Your activity in the app. The movies you rate, pick, save, or skip, and your preferences (services, mood, filters). We use this only to personalize your recommendations and remember your setup.

That's it. We do not collect your contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers for advertising.

How your data is used

To run the app: sign you in, sync your library, and tune the recommendations to your taste. Nothing more.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.
  • We do not share your data except with the service providers below that are required to run the app.

Service providers

  • Supabase stores your account and activity data securely on our behalf.
  • TMDB provides movie information. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

Children

Kids Mode is a filtered way to browse family-friendly movies. It does not create separate accounts and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Accounts are intended to be created by an adult.

Keeping and deleting your data

We keep your data while your account exists. You can ask us to delete your account and its data at any time by emailing the address below, and we will remove it.

Security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect it.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above. Continued use of the app means you accept the current version.
